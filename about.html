About Me

I'm a Nigerian creative technologist, currently based in London.

Tech is a medium for expression for me – I use it to communicate and bring things to life.

I also like people, fashion, reading, writing, languages, food, trapeze, parties, sunlight and music – and many other things.

I'm pragmatically optimistic, passionate, and I tend to overdo with my writing.
